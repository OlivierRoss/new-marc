New-Marc Research Inc. is an agricultural and environmental research company covering PMRA (Pest Management Regulatory Agency) / EPA (Environmental Protection Agency) zone 5. New-Marc Research is a (Standard Council of Canada) SCC-GLP recognized facilty (No 700) in the area of expertise of residue studies.

It is operating in 2 locations: St-Marc-sur-Richelieu, Quebec and Guelph, Ontario, Eastern Canada. Quebec office is managed by Robert Malouin, agronomist, who has over 12 years of experience in agricultural research. Ontario office is managed by Jamie Parnell, M.Sc Environmental Biology, who has 19 years of experience in agricultural research.

Our main goal is offering high quality contract research services for GLP and efficacy and confined (Biotech) studies to North American and International agricultural companies. At New-Marc Research farms, we conduct trials on a wide variety of crops such as Cash Crops, Vegetables, Pome and Stone fruits, Berries and many others.

New-Marc Research Inc. also works in cooperation with local growers allowing to conduct trials in different soil types, such as sandy, clay or muck soils
