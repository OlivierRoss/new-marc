New-Marc Research

 

 

New-Marc Research Inc. (formerly Atlantic Agritech Inc Quebec facility from 2005 to 2010) is an agricultural and environmental research company covering PMRA (Pest Management Regulatory Agency) / EPA (Environmental Protection Agency) zone 5. New-Marc Research Inc. is operating in St-Marc-sur-Richelieu, Quebec, Eastern Canada.

 

 

Our main goal is offering high quality contract research services for GLP and efficacy studies to North American agricultural companies. At New-Marc's farm, we conduct trials on a wide variety of crops; celery, lettuce, spinach, broccoli, cabbage, cauliflower, mustard green, cucumber, melon, squash, tomato, pepper, potato, carrot, beet, onion, corn, soybean, oat, barley, wheat and many others.

 

 

New-Marc Research Inc. also works in cooperation with local growers allowing us to conduct trials in different soil types (sandy soils or muck soils) and on different crops like blueberries, strawberries, etc.
